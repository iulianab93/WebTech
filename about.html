About

Spending time designing, producing & seeking my inspiration, literally exposed my soul in the beauty & serenity of nature. All the great splendour seemed to come over me; the delicate but robust and awe-inspiring brilliance of all beautiful things came over me like heavy rain that refreshes the earth & all things inside.

When we go through the healing process, and after a while, we look back, I believe we all carry the victory of our private shine. As experiences become discoveries, behind those harsh, often dark & even dangerous times, we develop & discover messages.

My intense, burning desire to create beauty in a world not always so beautiful, stems from trying to support those on the other side of the darkness who suffer domestic violence and aspire to survive. I need to share my message of courage, boldness & fearlessness for this purpose. Every design that we create or work with contains this powerful energy. That's why a portion of your transactions at SHINES help women and children escape the deadly darkness of domestic violence.

With love,
Julie

My brand is our reminder that we are courageous, strong and fearless. We can resolve difficulties in life, in every aspect of our being, regardless of what they may be & flourish on the other side to improve the lives of our children, families & society.
